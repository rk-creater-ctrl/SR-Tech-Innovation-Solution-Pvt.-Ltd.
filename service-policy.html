Service Policy

How SR Tech Innovation & Solution Pvt. Ltd. handles project inquiries, delivery, revisions, and support.

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Project Inquiry

Clients can request services through WhatsApp, the order form, email, or contact page. The first discussion is used to understand requirements, deadline, budget, and expected deliverables.

Scope Confirmation

Before work begins, SR Tech Innovation & Solution Pvt. Ltd. confirms the service type, pages or features, content requirements, timeline, and revision count. Any major feature added later may require a revised timeline or quote.

Delivery

Delivery may include website files, live live website links, software files, screenshots, documentation, or setup guidance depending on the project. Delivery timelines are estimates and depend on project complexity and client response time.

Revisions

Revisions cover reasonable changes within the agreed scope. New features, major redesigns, or extra modules are treated as additional work.

Support

Basic handover support is provided after delivery. Long-term maintenance, new changes, hosting support, or feature updates can be discussed separately.

Pricing and Add-ons

Package prices are starting estimates for clearly defined work. Domains, paid hosting, paid APIs, premium tools, payment gateways, urgent delivery, extra revisions, and larger feature requests may require a separate quote.

No Online Checkout

This website currently handles service inquiries only. Orders are discussed directly with SR Tech Innovation & Solution Pvt. Ltd. before any project is confirmed.
